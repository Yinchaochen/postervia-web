A warmer way to land in a city.

Postervia is designed for the moment when people do not need more tabs, forums, or random advice. They need a next step, a local signal, and sometimes another human.

1

Know what to do next

Odyssey turns bureaucracy, setup, and local life into a guided path with clear status and source context.

2

See the city through people

Plaza lets users share places, experiences, events, and practical notes that stay tied to real locations.

3

Ask for help without friction

Buddy and Social give newcomers a lighter path to companionship, errands, chat, and community trust.

Odyssey

A task path for life in a new city.

From SIM cards to housing confirmations, Odyssey keeps the important steps visible, ordered, and easy to act on.

  • Main and side tasks help users separate urgent needs from local exploration.
  • Source links and verification dates keep practical guidance grounded.
  • Completed and discarded paths are easy to restore from history.
Plaza

Local stories that can become next steps.

Plaza is a high-intent discovery feed for places, events, tips, warnings, and lived experience from the city.

  • Waterfall cards make the city feel visual, lively, and easy to scan.
  • Posts can include translation, location context, comments, and saves.
  • Useful places and events can be added back into a user's Odyssey.
Buddy

Human help for the moments that feel too heavy alone.

Buddy lets users ask for companionship, errands, and practical help around appointments, shopping, meals, and walking plans.

  • Request categories match real newcomer needs like Anmeldung and medical visits.
  • Users can make posts free, paid, or negotiable depending on the help needed.
  • The design keeps help requests clear, respectful, and easy to browse.
Social

Chat, friends, and groups without losing the practical context.

Postervia keeps social connection close to the city journey: saved notes, Buddy conversations, group plans, and lightweight search.

  • Friend search works around city, profile identity, and Postervia display IDs.
  • Message lists stay simple so users can find people and plans quickly.
  • Notifications bring users back to replies and community activity.
Profile and trust

A personal city identity, not another empty account.

Profiles show where people are based, what they share, how they explore, and which parts of their activity they want public.

  • Notes, comments, saves, and likes create a visible local archive.
  • Privacy controls help users decide which tabs other people can see.
  • Multilingual UI and translation keep the community open across language gaps.
Map and real places

Local discovery stays attached to the world.

Posts can be tied to actual shops, parks, events, and landmarks, so shared experience turns into usable local context.

  • Map tagging helps creators attach one or more real locations.
  • Place cards preserve addresses, context, and add-to-Odyssey actions.
  • Berlin is the first city, with the product shaped around city-by-city learning.

Built for people finding their footing.

Postervia starts in Berlin with guided tasks, local knowledge, and warm social help for newcomers, travelers, residents, and curious locals.
