Support

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Postervia is a community space for newcomers, residents, and travelers — especially women navigating life in a new city. If something isn't working, or you have a question about how to use the app, we're here to help.

Get in touch

Email: support@novaku.app

Typical response time: within 2 business days (Mon–Fri, Berlin time).

For account deletion or data-access requests under GDPR, see our privacy policy or write to the same address — we will route your request internally.

When you write in, please include:

  • Your account email (so we can find your account)
  • What you were trying to do
  • What went wrong (a screenshot or screen recording helps a lot)
  • Device + OS version (e.g. iPhone 14, iOS 18.4)

Common topics

Account & sign-in

  • Forgot password — On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and we'll email you a reset link.
  • Can't receive verification email — Check your spam folder. If still missing after 5 minutes, write to us and we can resend manually.
  • Want to delete your account — In the app, go to Profile → Settings → Privacy → Delete account. You can also email us; we'll process the request within 30 days and confirm by email.
  • Want a copy of your data — Same place: Profile → Settings → Privacy → Export my data. We'll email you a download link valid for 7 days.

Plaza & community posts

  • My post didn't publish — Posts that look like spam, contain abusive content, or appear to violate German law (e.g. reselling personal transit tickets) can be auto-rejected or flagged for review. If you believe this happened in error, email us with the post text.
  • How do I report a post or comment? — Tap the ••• menu on any post or comment, then Report. Reports go to our moderators within 24 hours.
  • How do I block another user? — Open their profile, tap •••, then Block. You won't see any of their posts or comments after that.

Buddy posts

  • What is a Buddy post? — A way to offer or ask for help (e.g. accompanying someone to a city office, or bringing back something from another city). Buddy posts are free to publish; in-app payment is not yet enabled in v1.
  • How do I contact a Buddy post author? — Open the post and tap "Contact this person" — it opens a one-to-one chat in the app.

Notifications & chat

  • Notifications aren't arriving — Check Profile → Settings → Notifications in the app, and that system-level notifications for Postervia are on in your phone's Settings.
  • Translation in chat — Tap the globe icon next to a message to translate it to your preferred language.

Subscription & billing

  • Where can I see my subscription? — Postervia+ is not enabled in the v1 launch. If a future release activates it, you'll find subscription management at Profile → Settings → Subscription, and the Apple/Google subscription page in your phone's system Settings.

Privacy & safety

  • I see content that violates the rules — Report it using the in-app report button. Critical issues (CSAM, threats) are escalated immediately; other reports are reviewed within 24 hours. See our Child Safety Standards for our CSAE policy and designated contact.
  • I think my account was compromised — Reset your password immediately, then email us so we can review recent activity on your account.

Still stuck?

Write to support@novaku.app with a description of the issue and a screenshot. We read every email.
